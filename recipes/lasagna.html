Lasagna Recipe

This is our families favorite lasagna recipe

This lasagna is typically made with gluten free noodles. It makes enough to feed our family of 7

The base recipe comes out a bit runny, which Allison likes. Dad prefers it to have spicy sausage and sit out a bit longer than normal, so it gets thicker.

Ingredient List

  • noodles
  • meat
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese!

Cooking Instructions

  1. cook the meat
  2. combine sauce and meat
  3. mix in cottage cheese
  4. layer noodles/sauce/noodles/sauce/noodles/sauce
  5. bake at 350 for 1.5 hours
  6. let stand 20 minutes before serving
